About PSP Optimize:

PSP Optimize is a portal for the PSP.

A portal is a collection of web pages, saved on your memory stick for offline use. With Flash support for the PSP enabled as of Firmware 2.7+, portals have started including Flash, enabling the development of games and applications.

PSP Optimize's navigation is fully HTML and CSS based. It is optimized for speed and convenience.
