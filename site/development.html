About the numbering system:

Example: 1.5b

A number change represents a major change. This can include a completely new layout and graphics, or the addition of major features. (ex: 2.0)

A decimal change represents a new version. This usually means new features or layout modifications. (ex: 1.2)

A "b" after the number indicates a beta version. (post 1.1)
